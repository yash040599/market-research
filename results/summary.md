## Assumptions

- Yahoo Finance daily auto-adjusted closes (split + dividend adjusted).

- Current NIFTY 50 membership held constant for the full 10y window (mild survivorship bias).

- ATH = running max of close prices observed within the backtest window.

- Fractional shares allowed; trade sized at Rs.10,000 notional at the buy-day close.

- Sell on the first close >= buy * (1 + Y/100); re-arm immediately for next dip from ATH.

- No brokerage / STT / slippage / liquidity caps.

- Open positions at end-of-window are marked-to-market on the final close as a synthetic XIRR inflow.

- Multiple stocks held simultaneously; no position-count cap; capital assumed available on demand.

- CAGR computed as (GrossReturned / MaxCapitalDeployed) ^ (1/years) - 1, where years spans first buy to last cashflow.

- Win-rate = % of trades (closed + open MTM) with positive P&L. Closed trades are 100% winners by construction.
